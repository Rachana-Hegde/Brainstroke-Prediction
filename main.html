Cardiology

Expert care for your heart and circulatory system.

The Cardiology department focuses on diagnosing and treating heart-related conditions. Our team provides expert care for issues like heart attacks, high blood pressure and irregular heartbeats, using advanced diagnostic tools and personalized treatment plans to ensure the best outcomes for patients.

Neurology

Comprehensive Brain and Nervous System Treatment

The Neurology department focuses on disorders affecting the brain, spine and nerves. We offer diagnosis and treatment for conditions like epilepsy, migraines, strokes and movement disorders. Our neurologists work closely with imaging and therapy teams to provide accurate care plans.

Hepatology

Expertise in Liver and Digestive Health

Our Hepatology department provides specialized and comprehensive care for a wide spectrum of liver diseases, including conditions like hepatitis, cirrhosis and fatty liver disease. We emphasize early detection and lifestyle-based management, alongside advanced medical therapies, ensuring thorough treatment for our patients.

Pediatrics

Compassionate Care for Children’s Health

The Pediatrics department supports children’s physical and emotional development from birth to adolescence. Our pediatricians provide essential vaccinations, nutritional guidance and effective treatment for both acute and chronic conditions, always with a family-centered approach to care.

Eye Care

Clear Vision Through Advanced Ophthalmology

Our Eye Care department offers a comprehensive range of services, from routine eye exams to advanced laser surgeries. We expertly diagnose and treat conditions like cataracts, glaucoma and vision loss, ensuring that patients of all ages receive personalized and effective eye care to maintain clear vision.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions and answers about our services to help you better understand what we offer and how we operate

How can I book an appointment with a specialist?

You can easily book an appointment through our website's 'Make an Appointment' section or by calling our reception desk directly during working hours. Please have your patient ID ready if you are a returning patient.

What insurance plans do you accept at your clinic?

We work with a wide range of insurance providers. For a complete list of accepted plans, please visit the 'Billing & Insurance' section of our website or feel free to contact our administrative staff who can assist you.

Do you offer emergency services or walk-in consultations?

While we primarily operate on an appointment basis, for immediate medical concerns, please visit our emergency department. For general consultations, we recommend booking in advance to ensure availability and minimize wait times.

Do you offer telemedicine or online consultations?

Yes, we provide convenient telemedicine consultations for follow-up appointments and certain medical conditions. This allows you to connect with our doctors from the comfort of your home. Please check our website for details.

What should I bring to my first appointment?

For your initial visit, please bring a valid ID, your insurance card, a list of any current medications you are taking and any relevant medical records or test results you may have. Arriving a few minutes early is also helpful.

How can I access my medical records or test results?

You can securely access your medical records and test results through our patient portal online. Our staff can assist you with setting up your account if you haven't already. Alternatively, you may request a physical copy.
